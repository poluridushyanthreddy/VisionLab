VisionLab

A modular computer vision platform. Upload an image, pick a model, and get AI-powered results . Choose a task from above.

Image Segmentation

Assigns labels to pixels in your image. Use this when you want to understand what is in the scene and where exactly it is.

YOLO Segmentation Instance

Detects each object separately and draws an individual mask around it. Great for images with multiple distinct objects — people, vehicles, animals, furniture.

DeepLabV3 Semantic

Labels every pixel by category without separating individual objects. Best for understanding whole scenes — roads, sky, buildings, vegetation.

Object Detection

Finds objects in your image and draws a bounding box around each one. Faster than segmentation and ideal when you just need locations.

YOLO Detection Bounding Box

Detects and localizes objects in real time. Optimized for speed — best when you need fast results without pixel-level masks.
